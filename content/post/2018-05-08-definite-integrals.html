Remembering some elementary calculus, we have \[\int_1^4 x^2 \, dx = \int_1^4 \frac{d}{dx} \frac{x^3}{3} \, dx = \left.\frac{x^3}{3}\right|_1^4 = \frac{4^3}{3}-\frac{1^3}{3} = 21\].
